Lenti-MPRA (LMPRA)

Lentivirus-based MPRA (lentiMPRA) produces “in-genome” readouts (M Grace Gordon et al., 2020)

  • Benefits
    • enables the use of MPRA in hard-to-transfect cells.
  • Reference
    • M Grace Gordon et al. (2020) lentiMPRA and MPRAflow for high-throughput functional characterization of gene regulatory elements. Nature protocols, 15, 2387–2412.
